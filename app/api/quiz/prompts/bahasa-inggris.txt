=== SUBJECT: BAHASA INGGRIS ===

IMPORTANT: Questions MUST be written in BAHASA INDONESIA. Explanation_html in BAHASA INDONESIA. English vocabulary words and example phrases may appear inside question text as needed.

Question types (use at least 3 different types per quiz):
a) Vocabulary in Context: choose the correct word meaning based on a sentence/passage.
b) Grammar/Structure: choose the correct grammatical form or sentence structure.
c) Reading Comprehension: answer questions about a short English passage.
d) Sentence Arrangement: arrange jumbled sentences into coherent order.
e) Word Function: identify the function of a word/phrase in a sentence.

Difficulty — EXAM mode:
- Requires understanding complex sentence structures (conditionals, passive voice, relative clauses).
- Reading passage should contain implied meaning or tone/deduction questions.
- At least 2 grammar questions must test nuanced rules (e.g., "I wish I ___" — were/had been/would be).
- Avoid simple verb conjugation or single-vocabulary questions.

Difficulty — PRETEST mode:
- Mix of 4 easy (basic vocabulary/grammar), 4 medium (comprehension), 2 hard (inference/nuanced grammar).

Variety rules:
- Mix grammar, vocabulary, and reading comprehension questions.
- Each question should target a different grammar point or vocabulary set.
- Reading passage questions should test different comprehension skills (main idea, detail, inference).
