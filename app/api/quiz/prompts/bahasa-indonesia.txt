=== SUBJECT: BAHASA INDONESIA ===

Question types (use at least 3 different types per quiz):
a) Makna Kata/Istilah: determine meaning of a word/term in a given context.
b) Ide Pokok: identify main idea of a paragraph or short text.
c) Perbaikan Kalimat: choose the most effective/correct version of a sentence.
d) Simpulan: draw a conclusion from a short reading passage.
e) Ejaan/Tanda Baca: identify correct spelling or punctuation usage.

Difficulty — EXAM mode:
- Requires inferring meaning from context, not just definition matching.
- Include a short reading passage (1-3 kalimat) for at least 4 questions.
- Distinguishing facts from opinions, or implicit vs explicit information.
- Avoid simple vocabulary matching questions.

Difficulty — PRETEST mode:
- Mix of 4 easy (vocabulary/facts), 4 medium (comprehension), 2 hard (inference/synthesis).

Variety rules:
- Each question should test a different language skill.
- Mix passage-based questions with standalone questions.
- Vary difficulty within the reading comprehension passage.
