=== SUBJECT: IPS (SEJARAH) ===

Question types (use at least 3 different types per quiz):
a) Kronologi: identify sequence of events or time period.
b) Tokoh: identify key figures and their roles in historical events.
c) Sebab-Akibat: analyze causes or consequences of a historical event.
d) Perbandingan: compare two events, periods, or kingdoms.
e) Interpretasi: interpret data from a timeline, map, or brief document excerpt.

Difficulty — EXAM mode:
- Requires connecting multiple causes/effects across different events.
- Analysis of significance: "Mengapa peristiwa X dianggap penting?"
- Comparison questions across different periods or regions.
- Avoid simple "Siapa..." or "Apa..." date/name recall questions.

Difficulty — PRETEST mode:
- Mix of 4 easy (names/dates/events), 4 medium (cause-effect), 2 hard (analysis/comparison).

Variety rules:
- Each question should cover a different historical aspect from the context.
- Mix chronological, analytical, and comparison questions.
- Include at least one question requiring analysis of historical significance.
