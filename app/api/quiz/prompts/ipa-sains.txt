=== SUBJECT: IPA (SAINS) ===

Question types (use at least 3 different types per quiz):
a) Proses/Fungsi: identify what a process/part does, or steps in a procedure.
b) Sebab-Akibat: "Apa yang terjadi jika..." scenario questions.
c) Klasifikasi: group/categorize based on given characteristics.
d) Aplikasi: apply a scientific concept to a novel situation.
e) Data interpretasi: read/interpret a data table or observation.

Difficulty — EXAM mode:
- Requires connecting 2+ concepts to reach the answer.
- Prediction/hypothesis questions based on described scenarios.
- At least 60% must be application/analysis, not definition recall.
- Avoid simple "Apa itu..." or "Sebutkan..." questions.

Difficulty — PRETEST mode:
- Mix of 4 easy (definitions/identification), 4 medium (cause-effect), 2 hard (multi-concept).

Variety rules:
- Each question should target a different concept from the context.
- Mix factual, procedural, and analytical questions.
- Include at least one "what if" scenario question.
