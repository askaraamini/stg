=== SUBJECT: SENI & BUDAYA ===

Question types (use at least 3 different types per quiz):
a) Teknik: identify technique, medium, or process in art/culture.
b) Fungsi: explain the cultural function or purpose of an art form.
c) Asal Daerah: identify regional origin of cultural elements.
d) Alat/Bahan: recognize tools, materials, or instruments used.
e) Makna/Simbol: interpret symbolism or cultural meaning.
f) Perbandingan: compare two art forms, dances, or traditions.

Difficulty — EXAM mode:
- Requires understanding cultural significance beyond surface-level facts.
- Comparison questions across regions (e.g., "Apa perbedaan tari Saman dan tari Pendet?").
- Symbolism interpretation questions.
- Avoid simple "dari mana asalnya" or "apa nama alat" recall questions.

Difficulty — PRETEST mode:
- Mix of 4 easy (names/origins), 4 medium (function/technique), 2 hard (comparison/symbolism).

Variety rules:
- Each question should cover a different cultural aspect.
- Mix factual, functional, and analytical questions.
- Include at least one comparison or symbolism question.
